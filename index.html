About

Engineering data, delivering clarity.

I'm a Data Analyst and Data Engineer with an engineering degree in Data Science & IoT from ENSIAS, one of Morocco's leading engineering schools. Today I work at Attijari Payment in Casablanca, where I sit at the intersection of data engineering, business intelligence and machine learning in the fast-moving payments industry.

My work covers the full data lifecycle: building distributed ETL pipelines with PySpark, structuring data layers for advanced analytics, automating executive reporting with Power BI and Power Automate, and applying machine learning to real business problems - from cardholder segmentation to ATM cash-demand forecasting with ARIMAX.

What sets my approach apart is a strong grounding in business needs. I don't just move data: I contribute to the data roadmap and analytics planning of the organization, making sure every pipeline, model and dashboard answers a real performance question. I work fluently in English, French and Arabic, and I'm comfortable in Agile/Scrum teams.
